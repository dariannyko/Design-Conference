Lost the signal with inspiration? Plug into the outlet again together with a large design community!

Our task is to provide information on three key areas: Attracting customers: how to ensure the company's workload by 120%. Business: how the process is organized in an effective studio. A systematic approach to work organization, delegation, quality control and creativity. Subtleties and secrets of professional work: design, photography, offices, landscape design, socionics, lean management, 3D visualization, design, design and construction of wooden cottages.
